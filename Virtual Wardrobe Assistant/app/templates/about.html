About Virtual Wardrobe Assistant

The Virtual Wardrobe Assistant is a modern solution for organizing your clothing collection, planning outfits, and maximizing your wardrobe usage.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help people make the most of their existing wardrobes, reduce unnecessary purchases, and find joy in their personal style. By providing tools to catalog, organize, and plan outfits, we aim to simplify daily clothing decisions while promoting sustainable fashion practices.

Key Features

Clothing Catalog

Digitize your entire wardrobe with detailed information about each item, including images, categories, colors, and when you last wore them.

Outfit Planning

Create outfit combinations from your clothing items and organize them for different occasions, seasons, or weather conditions.

Weather-Based Suggestions

Get outfit recommendations based on the current weather conditions in your location, ensuring you're always dressed appropriately.

Usage Tracking

Track how often you wear each item to identify favorites and rarely worn pieces, helping you make better decisions about future purchases.

How It Works

The Virtual Wardrobe Assistant uses advanced algorithms to suggest outfits based on several factors:

  • Weather Conditions: Suggests appropriate clothing based on current temperature and precipitation.
  • Occasion: Recommends outfits suitable for specific events or settings (casual, work, formal, etc.).
  • Wear History: Prioritizes items that haven't been worn recently to ensure rotation.
  • Color Coordination: Ensures that suggested outfit combinations are aesthetically pleasing.
